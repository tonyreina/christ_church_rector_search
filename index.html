Welcome to Christ Church

We are a friendly, inclusive Episcopal community in Coronado, California. Our parish is home to over 200 members from all walks of life, including many military families. We value tradition, thoughtful worship, and caring for one another. Our motto "Bridging Love and Service" reflects our desire to grow a spiritual community in harmony with our surrounding secular community.

Christ Church Day School, founded in 1957, is the only Episcopal elementary school in the Diocese of San Diego. We are committed to its future and to Christian education for all ages.

We seek a mature leader trusted to work collaboratively with vestry, lay leaders, school, and staff.

We welcome all people and look forward to meeting our next rector. If you feel called, we hope to hear from you.

— The People of Christ Church
