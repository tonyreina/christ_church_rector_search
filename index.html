Welcome

Welcome to Christ Church

We are a friendly, inclusive Episcopal community in Coronado, California. Our parish is home to over 200 members from all walks of life, including many military families. We value tradition, thoughtful worship, and caring for one another.

Christ Church Day School, founded in 1957, is the only Episcopal elementary school in the Diocese of San Diego. We are committed to its future and to Christian education for all ages.

Our hopes for our next rector
  • Grow our youth and family ministry
  • Welcome new members and deepen faith
  • Support small groups and learning
  • Offer caring support in all seasons
  • Lead us in service and outreach
  • Provide faithful stewardship and care

We welcome all people and look forward to meeting our next rector. If you feel called, we hope to hear from you.

— The People of Christ Church
