Youth and Family Ministry

  • We welcome children, teens, and families to grow in faith together.
  • Sunday School and Youth Group offer learning, friendship, and service opportunities for all ages.
  • Kids Praise is our children’s choir.
  • Sunday Supper is a monthly gathering with worship and dinner for all generations.
  • Camp Create is our summer Vacation Bible School.
From our Ministry Profile

Growing youth formation

We are encouraged by new life in our ministries: our youth programs are rebuilding with the hiring of Leighton Jones, our new Youth Minister and Day School chaplain. We are nurturing the next generation through thoughtful Christian formation that invites wonder, questions, and belonging.

Our vision for youth and family ministry includes:

  • Regular Sunday School and youth gatherings that build friendships rooted in faith
  • Godly Play sessions that help children encounter scripture through story and wonder
  • Service opportunities that connect young people to Christ's mission in the world
  • Intergenerational worship and fellowship that strengthens the whole parish family
  • Deepening partnerships with Christ Church Day School for shared formation and worship
  • Creating space where young people feel genuinely seen, heard, and valued
  • Supporting parents and families as they nurture faith at home
