Christ Church Day School

Christ Church Day School (CCDS) is a vital and important mission of Christ Church. Our Episcopal Day School is fully accredited through the Western Association of Schools and Colleges and has been providing quality academic education with a focus on Christian values since 1957. With one class per grade serving junior kindergarten through seventh grade students with a plan to expand to grade eight in the next school year, the current enrollment is 130 students.

With small class sizes, the Day School provides all students with rich specialty programs that include classes in Spanish, Music, Physical Education, Art, and Spiritual Nurture. Christ Church Day School hosts twice weekly all-school chapels with Eucharist on Tuesday and Morning Prayer on Thursdays. CCDS is governed by a board that includes members from the parish, vestry, and school parents. The Day School partners frequently with the church on service projects, including our annual Spaghetti Dinner and Thanksgiving Food Drive.

The Day School keeps the church and school grounds alive and vibrant with open houses, back-to-school events, an annual Harvest Festival, move night on the lawn, music and talent performances, classroom family night highlighting student projects, math and game nights, pet blessings, Scholastic Book Fairs, and an end-of-the-year play day.

Our parish-school partnership

Our partnership with Christ Church Day School is a treasured and defining part of our parish identity. For over 70 years, we have shared our campus, our worship life, and our commitment to Christian formation with students and families who bring energy, diversity, and joy to our community.

We believe this partnership strengthens both church and school. Students participate in weekly chapels, building early connections to Episcopal worship and tradition. Parents and faculty often join parish events, deepening relationships across generations. Service projects unite school families and parishioners in common mission. Our Day School chaplain serves both communities, weaving together formation, pastoral care, and spiritual nurture.

We care for our 130-year-old campus as a gift entrusted to us by past generations and held in trust for those to come. As we plan responsibly for maintenance, growth, and future needs, we do so knowing that this sacred space serves not only Sunday worship but also the daily formation of young hearts and minds throughout the week.

Christ Church Day School (ccds.org)

A thriving Episcopal school in the heart of Coronado

Christ Church Day School is a small private Episcopal school serving Junior Kindergarten through 8th grade. The school’s mission is to engage students to reason, create, serve, and lead in a nurturing, inclusive, Episcopal learning community. Students also participate in Spiritual Nurture classes and gather for Holy Eucharist and Community Chapel during the school week.

The school-wide virtues that shape community life include belonging, respect, responsibility, patience, kindness, forgiveness, honesty, compassion, and generosity.
