Christ Church Day School

A thriving Episcopal school in the heart of Coronado

Christ Church Day School (CCDS) is a vital mission of Christ Church, serving JK–8th grade with 130 students in small classes. Fully accredited and founded in 1957, CCDS provides quality education grounded in Christian values.

Enrollment
130 students, JK–8
Class sizes
Small (about 14–16)
Founded
1957

Students receive rich specialty programs including Spanish, Music, Physical Education, Art, and Spiritual Nurture. CCDS hosts twice-weekly all-school chapels with Eucharist on Tuesdays and Morning Prayer on Thursdays.

The school-wide virtues that shape community life include belonging, respect, responsibility, patience, kindness, forgiveness, honesty, compassion, and generosity.

CCDS keeps the campus alive and vibrant with open houses, back-to-school events, an annual Harvest Festival, movie nights on the lawn, music and talent performances, family nights, pet blessings, Scholastic Book Fairs, and end-of-year celebrations. The school partners frequently with the church on service projects, including our annual Spaghetti Dinner and Thanksgiving Food Drive.
