Christ Church Day School

Christ Church Day School (CCDS) is a vital and important mission of Christ Church. Our Episcopal Day School is fully accredited through the Western Association of Schools and Colleges and has been providing quality academic education with a focus on Christian values since 1957. With one class per grade serving junior kindergarten through seven grade students with a plan to expand to grand eight in the next school year, the current enrollment is 130 students. In addition to to small class sizes, the Day School provides all students with a rich specialty programs that include classes in Spanish, Music, Physical Education, Art, and Spiritual Nurture. Christ Church Day School hosts twice weekly all school chapels with Eucharist on Tuesday and Morning Prayer on Thursdays. CCDS is governed by a board that includes members from the parish, vestry, and school parents. The Day School partners frequently with the church on service projects, including our annual Spaghetti Dinner and Thanksgiving Food Drive.

The Day School keeps the church and school grounds alive and vibrant with open houses, back-to-school events, an annual Harvest Festival, move night on the lawn, music and talent performances, classroom family night highlighting student projects, math and game nights, pet blessings, Scholastic Book Fairs, and an end-of-the-year play day.

Our parish-school partnership

Christ Church is preparing for the future with hope, openness, and a commitment to welcoming all of God’s children. We support worship and pastoral care through dedicated parishioners, strengthened by clergy guidance, and we offer both Rite I and Rite II so people can worship in the tradition that best nourishes them. We grow engagement through active ministries and committees, intentionally inviting new voices into shared leadership and discernment. We also work to be visible in the wider community, so our neighbors know who we are and how we seek to follow Jesus. Our partnership with Christ Church Day School is a treasured part of our identity, and we are strengthening youth formation through Godly Play, fellowship, and meaningful learning. We care for our 132-year old campus as a gift entrusted to us, planning responsibly for maintenance and future needs. Finally, we seek deeper relationships across the Diocese, especially with parishes serving immigrant communities, so we can stand with those most in need.

Christ Church Day School (ccds.org)

A thriving Episcopal school in the heart of Coronado

Christ Church Day School is a small private Episcopal school serving Junior Kindergarten through 8th grade. The school’s mission is to engage students to reason, create, serve, and lead in a nurturing, inclusive, Episcopal learning community. Students also participate in Spiritual Nurture classes and gather for Holy Eucharist and Community Chapel during the school week.

The school-wide virtues that shape community life include belonging, respect, responsibility, patience, kindness, forgiveness, honesty, compassion, and generosity.
