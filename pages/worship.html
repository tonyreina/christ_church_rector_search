Welcome to Christ Church Coronado. We are a friendly, inclusive community where everyone is invited to worship and grow in faith.

All are welcome at Christ's table. Whether you are new, returning, or seeking, you are invited to join us.

Weekly Worship Schedule:

  • Sunday 8:00 am – Holy Eucharist (Rite I)
  • Sunday 10:00 am – Holy Eucharist (Rite II, choir & organ)
  • Tuesday 8:00 am – Holy Eucharist (Rite II, Day School Chapel)
  • Thursday 8:00 am – Morning Prayer (with Class Chaplains)
  • Wednesday 5:30 pm – Evening Prayer (lay led)

Most of our members value our traditional faith, but some would like more variety in worship, prayers, and music.

Ministry Profile

Worship Style

Our worship blends tradition and flexibility. We use Rite I and Rite II, enjoy singing, and offer a mix of musical styles. Services are welcoming and not rigid. We have weekly chapels for our day school and lay-led evening prayer. Many hope to bring back our Thursday healing service.
