Thrift Shop

“Joining God’s Work in the World”

One of the most significant and impactful ministries of Christ Church is our Thrift Shop. In operation for over 50 years, it is managed and staffed entirely by nearly 50 volunteers. 100% of the proceeds support a wide range of parish activities and outreach efforts at the diocesan, national, and international levels. In 2025 alone, over $100,000 in funds were provided to worthwhile ministries, including Christ Church Day School, the San Diego Food Bank, World Food Kitchen, PAWS (our local animal welfare group), Vida Joven (a foundation that supports orphanages in Tijuana), The Episcopal Refugee Network, and various other projects of The Episcopal Diocese of San Diego.

Beyond its vital financial outreach, the Thrift Shop provides help to low-income families by offering quality merchandise at very low cost. It also creates fellowship for volunteers and shoppers who’ve developed relationships over the years. Volunteer managers and workers come not only from our church, but from the Coronado community at large. The shop is open for sales and donations on Tuesday through Saturday, 10 am to 1 pm.

The Thrift Shop embodies our call to service in tangible, practical ways, connecting commerce with compassion and stewardship with generosity.
